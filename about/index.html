The short version

Atlanta roots, analytical approach.

I'm Joshua — an Atlanta REALTOR® with Red 1 Realty. I work across Intown and the broader metro, with a focus on giving clients the same quality of information I'd want if I were sitting on their side of the table.

My practice leans on three habits I don't cut corners on: verified data, clear communication, and calm negotiation. Real estate is emotional. My job is to keep it from becoming chaotic.

Outside of real estate, I'm a relentless neighborhood-walker, a coffee-shop regular in Virginia-Highland, and a believer that the best deal is the one both sides walk away feeling good about.

How I work

Four commitments to every client.

01

Verified information, always

Square footage, permitted work, tax history, school zones — cross-checked before we ever book a showing. Misreported data has sunk too many closings; it won't sink ours.

02

Responsive communication

Weekday texts and emails answered in under an hour. You'll know where we stand, what's next, and why — in plain English.

03

Negotiation without theater

I'd rather win you a fair deal than waste a weekend on a game of chicken. Strategy first, ego never.

04

Discretion and trust

Your finances, timeline, and motivations stay between us. No leaks to the other side of the table, no social-media reveals without your say.

Service area

Where I work.

Decatur & Kirkwood
