About this home

A cleanly-renovated Midtown craftsman on a generous lot, a short walk from Piedmont Park and the Beltline. The main level opens through a central foyer into a living room with the original fireplace preserved; pocket doors lead to a study with custom shelving. The kitchen has been reimagined with quartz counters, paneled appliances, and a walk-in pantry.

Upstairs, the primary suite runs the depth of the house, with dual walk-in closets and a marble-wrapped bath. Two additional bedrooms share a hall bath; a fourth bedroom / bonus room sits above the detached two-car garage.

The screened back porch and fenced yard make the rear of the home the heart of the house in warmer months. A new HVAC (2024), updated roof (2023), and tankless water heater round out the major systems.

Location

Midtown Atlanta is the city's cultural and commercial heart — a dense, walkable neighborhood bounded by Piedmont Park to the east and the Connector to the west. The home is a five-minute walk to the park, ten minutes to the High Museum, and a short drive to Georgia Tech, Emory, and downtown.
